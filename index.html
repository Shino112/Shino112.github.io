Faculty information and projects

Student of the last year at the University of Rijeka. I study IT in the Department of Informatics. The name of my module is Information and Communication systems. During my time at college I was doing many things. I was doing experiments on machine learning with Python and R. I made a Sudoku game for Android OS with Android Studio and Java programming language.

In collaboration with two of my collegaues I have made a web page for ordering food. The page was made with PHP framework Laravel. For my last project I built this website with GitHub Pages.

Work

Since I was in middle school I started working in Valamar Riviera during summer holiday. After two years I started working in the Department of Reservation. I was working with Hotel hospitality applications: PMS, Channel Manager.

During my last three years I have been the administrator of their Sharepoint site. I manage the content and design for the site and also create application tutorials for the company and e-learning projects.

About me

I am 23 years old, for now. I live in Poreč, Istria. I have many hobbies and practice many sports. I have been playing table tennis for 13 years for my club STK Jadran Poreč. Four years ago I also started playing tennis, I am a proud member of TK Pazin.

In my free time I play cards with my friends(cro. briškula and cro. trešete) and play PES. When the winter season starts I go skiing in Austria and Italy few times in a year.
